The Science of Muscle Gain

Muscle gain, or hypertrophy, occurs when muscle fibers undergo microscopic damage from resistance training and recover to become stronger and thicker. Key elements include:

  • Progressive Overload: Gradually increasing resistance or volume.
  • Protein Synthesis: Building new muscle proteins faster than theyâ€™re broken down.
  • Recovery: Muscles grow during rest, not during workouts.
  • Nutrition: Adequate protein, carbs, and calories are essential.
