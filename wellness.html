The Science of Wellness

Wellness is a holistic approach to health that involves physical, mental, and emotional well-being. The core principles of wellness include:

  • Balanced Nutrition: Eating a variety of nutrient-rich foods to support health and energy.
  • Physical Activity: Regular movement improves heart health, mobility, and mood.
  • Mental Health: Stress management, mindfulness, and emotional support are vital.
  • Sleep & Recovery: Adequate rest allows the body and mind to reset and heal.
  • Hydration: Proper water intake is essential for every body system.
