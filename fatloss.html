The Science of Fat Loss

Fat loss happens when your body uses stored fat for energy due to a calorie deficit. The key elements of fat loss include:

  • Caloric Deficit: Consuming fewer calories than your body burns.
  • Metabolism: The rate at which your body burns calories at rest and during activity.
  • Exercise: Strength training preserves muscle, and cardio increases energy expenditure.
  • Diet Quality: Whole foods, high protein, and fiber support satiety and fat loss.

Supplements for Fat Loss

L-Carnitine

Helps transport fatty acids into cells to be used for energy.

Whey Protein

Helps preserve lean muscle mass and promotes satiety during a fat-loss diet.

Creatine Monohydrate

Supports strength during resistance training, which helps maintain muscle while losing fat.
